Note: If you are a consumer seeking a retailer where you can purchase OpenDocs books, please visit the "Where to buy" page.
General Information
All bookstores and retail establishments can now purchase OpenDocs Publications through Baker and Taylor. Baker and Taylor can be found on the web at: http://www.btol.com/. We strongly suggest that retailers that have a relationship with Baker and Taylor utilize the existing distribution channel.

If you have an existing account with OpenDocs, LLC. you may order new inventory from us by faxing a Purchase Order to:

OpenDocs, LLC. (503) 963-8316

If you prefer you may email a purchase order to books@opendocs.org.

If you would like to open an account with OpenDocs, you must first remit a standard credit reference sheet. This credit sheet must include a minimum of 3 credit references and must be completed in full before any purchases will be approved. To remit the credit sheet please fax the completed form to (503) 588-8376, or email the form to us. Upon credit approval you will be contacted by one of our represenatives to set up your account and help you with your first order.

If you have any questions about this process please contact us at books@opendocs.org

Return Policies
Overstock Returns
All books purchased through OpenDocs must be kept for a minimum of 120 days before an overstock return can be processed. In order to receive a credit for the overstock, books must be returned in saleable condition.

Out Of Print Returns

When a book has be declared "Out of Print" you have up to 180 days to return remaining stock. In order to receive a credit for "Out of Print" books they must be returned in saleable condition.

Defect Returns

If you receive merchandise that you feel is defective (torn covers, missing CD's etc..) please contact OpenDocs immediately to make new product arrangements.
