Author Information
Write for OpenDocs Publishing
Over the year 2000, OpenDocs, LLC has learned many things. One of the things that we have learned is that many people have ideas for books but never present them. Well, here is your chance. If you are an author, or a would-be author, we want to hear from you.

Our ideal author has a desire to write in a clear and effective manner. We expect complete competence in the subject matter. As the technical industry changes rapidly we are happy to work with first-time authors to demonstrate a desire to produce a quality manuscript.

We are open to any ideas on technical books that you may have, but have particular interest in the following areas:

  • FreeBSD
  • Linux
  • Open Source in General
  • Programming Languages (specifically Python, Perl, and PHP)
  • Databases (including MySQL and PostgreSQL)

If you have an idea in mind for any of the above or any other technical book, please do not hesitate to contact us at books@opendocspublishing.com
